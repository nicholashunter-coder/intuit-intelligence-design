## What's Next

### Web Source Enrichment

The highest priority improvement is resolving **favicons and company names** for web sources. V1 currently shows raw URLs, which makes it harder for customers to quickly assess source credibility.

### Clarity Expiry Mitigation

QB report links expire after ~15 minutes due to the Clarity platform constraint. We're exploring options to either extend the expiry window or provide a seamless re-generation flow so customers don't hit dead links.

### Source Relevance

Currently sources are grouped by category (QB first, then Web). Future iterations may rank sources by relevance to the specific answer, surfacing the most important source first regardless of category.
